# Empathy Map
### StockPulse — Trading Platform Simulation
**Phase 1: Brainstorming & Ideation**

---

## Target User: Beginner Investor / CS Student

**User Persona:** Riya, 21 years old, Computer Science student who wants to learn stock trading before investing real money.

---

## Empathy Map

### 👁️ SAYS
> "I want to learn how the stock market works but I'm scared of losing real money."

> "I don't understand all the financial terms and charts."

> "I wish there was a way to just try it out without risk."

> "My friends are investing but I don't know where to start."

> "I need something that looks real so I can actually practice."

---

### 🧠 THINKS
- "What if I make a wrong decision and lose everything?"
- "Is this even worth learning? It seems too complicated."
- "I wish someone could guide me step by step."
- "Real trading apps feel overwhelming — too many numbers and charts."
- "I want to see how my portfolio would grow over time."

---

### 👀 SEES
- Friends talking about stocks on social media
- Complex trading platforms with hundreds of indicators
- News about people making (and losing) huge amounts of money
- Ads for investment apps that require real money upfront
- College courses about finance with no practical component

---

### 🎯 DOES
- Googles "how to start investing" but gets overwhelmed
- Watches YouTube videos about stock trading
- Downloads apps but quits when real money is required
- Talks to friends about investing but never takes action
- Tries free apps but finds them too simplified to be useful

---

### 😤 PAINS
| Pain | Severity |
|------|----------|
| Fear of losing real money | High |
| Overwhelming UI on real platforms | High |
| No guided onboarding for beginners | High |
| Static / fake prices in existing simulators | Medium |
| No portfolio performance tracking | Medium |
| No transaction history to learn from | Medium |
| Lack of a step-by-step learning path | High |

---

### 🎉 GAINS
| Desired Gain | How StockPulse Solves It |
|---|---|
| Risk-free practice environment | $100,000 virtual currency — no real money |
| Realistic experience | Real-time price updates via WebSockets |
| Clean & simple interface | Robinhood-inspired redesign, minimal clutter |
| Learning from mistakes | Full transaction history + activity logs |
| Portfolio performance visibility | Insights dashboard with profit/loss tracking |
| Guided onboarding | Step-by-step How to Trade guide page |
| Sense of progress | Balance display, portfolio growth metrics |

---

## Key Insights from Empathy Mapping

1. **Fear is the #1 barrier** — Users want to try but are afraid. StockPulse removes this by making everything virtual.

2. **Simplicity matters** — Beginners are put off by complex UIs. The Robinhood-inspired design keeps it clean and approachable.

3. **Realism builds confidence** — If the simulation feels real (live prices, real stock names, real charts), users will trust their learning.

4. **Guidance is essential** — A step-by-step guide page bridges the gap between "how do I start?" and "I placed my first trade."

5. **Feedback drives engagement** — Showing profit/loss, transaction logs, and portfolio growth keeps users engaged and learning.
